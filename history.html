The History of Ancient Egypt

Ancient Egypt's history spans over 3,000 years, starting around 3100 BCE with its founding.

Egypt was divided into the Old, Middle, and New Kingdoms. Key figures include Pharaohs like Djoser, Khufu, and Ramses II.
