Early Life of Ramses II

Ramses II was born around 1303 BCE to Pharaoh Seti I and Queen Tuya. As the crown prince, he was given military responsibilities at a young age, honing his skills in leadership and battle tactics.

Ramses became co-ruler with his father in his teenage years, preparing him to assume the throne. His upbringing was carefully crafted to establish him as a powerful ruler.
