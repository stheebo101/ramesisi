The Culture of Ancient Egypt

Egyptian culture was rich in art, music, and religious practices. They are known for their complex rituals and contributions to science and the arts.

Egyptian society was highly structured, with the Pharaoh at the top. Art and architecture were deeply connected to religion, and they made advancements in literature, medicine, and mathematics.
