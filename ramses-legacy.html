Legacy of Ramses II

Ramses II’s legacy endures as one of Egypt's most revered leaders. He is often remembered for his monumental constructions and contributions to Egypt’s strength and prosperity.

His mummy was discovered in 1881 and remains a significant artifact, symbolizing his everlasting impact on Egypt and world history.
